A searchable side-by-side comparison of the local rules of North Carolina's Eastern, Middle, and Western federal district courts, so practitioners can spot procedural differences without juggling three PDFs.

A curated collection of Claude prompts for legal professionals, organized by practice area for tasks like case summaries, memo drafting, contract review, and document editing.
